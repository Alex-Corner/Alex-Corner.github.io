Alex Corner

Sheffield Hallam University


I am a lecturer in the mathematics subject group at Sheffield Hallam University and first year tutor for BSc Mathematics. My research interests are in category theory and mathematics pedagogy in higher education. Within category theory I am particularly interested in monoidal categories, higher categories, and operads.

Previously

I completed my undergraduate MMath at the University of Sheffield, completing a project on abelian categories. I then went on to complete a PhD under the supervision of Nick Gurski. Throughout this time I was involved in teaching within the School of Mathematics and Statistics, as well as the International College.
